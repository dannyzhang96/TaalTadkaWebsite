ABOUT

Taal Tadka is the first and only South Asian fusion a cappella group from Georgia Tech! We perform medleys of songs in Hindi, English, and other South Asian languages as well. As our name suggests, we believe in creating "spiced-up rhythm" using just our voices, and aim to bring cultures together in the process!

Take a look at what we do!

Taal Tadka performs several times during the year on the Georgia Tech campus, around the Atlanta area, and other locations throughout the country. We also hold open auditions every August and January for Atlanta-area college students (both undergraduate and graduate). Check our calendar for important performance and audition dates!

We have also released several recorded tracks, and last year we released our first album! Check out our Spotify/iTunes/YouTube for all our tracks and music videos!
